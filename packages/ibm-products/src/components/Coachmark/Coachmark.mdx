## Overview

[Coachmarks](https://pages.github.ibm.com/cdai-design/pal/novice-to-pro/components/coachmark/usage)
are just-in-time messages used to draw a userâ€™s attention and explain new
functionality or concepts they might not otherwise notice or understand.

**Note:**

- The target prop should always be either a CoachmarkBeacon or a
  CoachmarkButton.
- BEACON_KIND, COACHMARK_OVERLAY_KIND, COACHMARK_ALIGNMENT are enums exported as
  a convenience from Coachmark.

_[Novice to pro](https://pages.github.ibm.com/security/security-design/teams-across-security/novice-to-pro/overview/)
is a continuous learning methodology + framework aimed to orient, onboard,
explain, educate, and cultivate novice users into high-functioning power users._

## Note on theming

The Coachmark utilizes the theme prop (E.g. `theme='dark'`) to set the
appropriate background and font colors for the overlay. Specify a value ('light'
or 'dark') to display properly within your usage.
